ABOUT THE World Cup

The World Cup in football (soccer) is a tournament of 32 national teams that is organized by the Fédération Internationale de Football Association also known as(FIFA). This sport is designed to host the best countries, to compete in an all out fight for the most prestigious trophy in world football. Aside of the Uefa Champion League tournament. It is the most popular sporting event in the world, drawing billions of television viewers every tournament.

Motivation

I was motivated to do this project because I love football. My favorite team is Liverpool FC which is based in England. The country I support is the country I was born in, United States of America. The league in the US isn’t that popular or strong as compared to the other top five leagues around the world. In the US we have the MLS but top 5 most viewed leagues are the Premier League, La Liga, Serie A, Bundesliga, and Ligue 1. There are many other leagues around the world that have players that participate in the world cup but the majority of the star players come from these top five leagues. These star players end up playing for their national team hoping to win the world cup. Having already watched a few world cups in my lifetime, I thought that it would be a fun experience for myself to showcase my love and appreciation to the sport and what better way is it than to discuss and visualize the best tournament in the world.
